Hey Developers !

As you all know Hacktoberfest 2024 is here, and we are so excited to welcome you all to our community, levelupdev, and we are accepting contributions to our open source project. More details below on how to participate. 


[![Contribute to Hacktoberfest](/images/hacktoberfest.png)](/images/hacktoberfest.png)

# About Hacktoberfest 2024

Every year, during October (the entire month) Digital Ocean and other sponsors, organise a month-long celebration of open source software called Hacktoberfest. The goal is to encourage participation in the open source community and to help developers get started with contributing to open source projects.

# How to participate in Hacktoberfest 2024

- Head over to [Hacktoberfest 2024 website](https://hacktoberfest.com/) and register yourself
- Find open source projects to contribute to with the <strong>`hacktoberfest`</strong> label. You can find these projects on [GitHub](https://github.com/topics/hacktoberfest) or [GitLab](https://go.gitlab.com/ubCLKL)
- Create 4 PR's to any open source projects (Hint - You can make your contributions to levelupdev project repo)
- Get them approved and merged successfully

# How to contribute to levelupdev project

[![Contribute to levelupdev](/images/contribute-bg.jpeg)](/images/contribute-bg.jpeg)

- Head over to [levelupdev project repo](https://github.com/sujaykundu777/levelupdev)
- Fork the repo and clone it to your local machine
- Create a new branch and make your changes
- Push your changes to your forked repo
- Create a PR to the main repo
- Get your PR approved and merged successfully

# What to contribute to levelupdev project

[![Contribute to levelupdev](/images/levelupdev-hacktoberfest.png)](/images/levelupdev-hacktoberfest.png)


- You can contribute to the project by adding new features, fixing bugs, improving the documentation, etc.
- You can also contribute by writing blog posts for our community blog.
- Check the issues tab in the repo to find the issues you can work on and contribute to. Marked with the `hacktoberfest` label.
- You can also contribute by creating new issues, and helping others to resolve their issues.

# What you get in return

- You get a chance to win some cool swags from Digital Ocean and other sponsors
- You get a chance to learn new things and improve your skills
- You get a chance to be a part of the levelupdev community
- Goodies from levelupdev community
- Get a chance to be featured in our community blog
- Be a part of the contributors list in the project repo

So what are you waiting for ? Head over to [Hacktoberfest 2024 website](https://hacktoberfest.com/) and register yourself and start contributing to levelupdev project.
